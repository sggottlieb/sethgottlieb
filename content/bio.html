Equal parts developer, architect, and team builder, Seth transforms ideas into successful businesses. As a software development manager at Amazon, Seth leads teams to develop capabilities for the Alexa service.

Seth draws from a deep background in software development and integration. For more than 20 years, he has led consulting and in-house project teams and built performance-oriented engineering cultures.

Prior product development roles at Amazon, Aberdeen, and Lionbridge, Seth worked as an integrator, consultant, and industry analyst specializing in content technologies. As content management practice lead at Optaros and founder of Content Here, he established himself as an authority on CM architecture and open source software. During this time, Seth was a frequent speaker content management conferences and prolific writer of software evaluations and articles for publications such as CMS Wire, Cutter IT Journal, and ZDNet. Seth also served on the Board of Directors for Content Management Professionals.
